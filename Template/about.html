About Us

Established in 2015, Foundation for the helpless caters to the basic needs of impoverished communities in areas of South-Eastern Nigeria. Our food distribution to the communities is done with the understanding that a malnourished body can provide an easy home for disease and illness. It gives us complete satisfaction to witness great show of appreciation on the faces of the people we serve. Some community members rely on us for their only supply of meat and protein during the entire year. Foundation for the helpless is a 501(c)(3) IRS-approved nonprofit entity. Consequently, all donations are tax-deductible to the full extent of the law.

Help Fight Hunger and Poverty. Your tax-deductible support and contribution enable us to meet our goals and improve conditions.
